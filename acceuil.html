DENTIST

The dental surgeon, also called a dental doctor1 or more simply a dentist, is a medical health professional authorized to practice odontology. Holder of a state diploma of doctor of dental surgery or dental medicine in most countries, he ensures the prevention, diagnosis and treatment of congenital or acquired anomalies and diseases, real or supposed, of the mouth, teeth, jawbones and adjoining tissues2.
